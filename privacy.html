Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 26, 2025

Surya Plaza Hotel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our guests' personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, stay at our hotel, or use our services. Your use of our services constitutes your acceptance of the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide and enhance our services. This may include:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number, nationality, government-issued identification (for check-in compliance), and accommodation preferences or special requests.
  • Automatically Collected Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and cookie data when you interact with our website.

2. Use of Your Information

Your data is used for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • To manage and process your reservations.
  • To provide personalized customer service and send booking confirmations.
  • To improve our website, services, and guest experience.
  • To send occasional promotional communications, should you opt-in.
  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure the safety of our guests.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third parties under strict data protection standards, including:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors and booking engine partners who facilitate our services.
  • Legal Authorities: Government or law enforcement agencies when required by law.

4. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements. You may also opt-out of marketing communications at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact our Privacy Officer via the details provided below.

5. Data Security

We employ robust technical and administrative measures to protect your information, including SSL encryption, secure servers, controlled data access, and regular security reviews. While no system is entirely foolproof, we take all reasonable steps to safeguard your data.

6. Data Retention & Cookies

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations. Our website uses cookies—small text files stored on your device—to remember your preferences and improve website performance. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time.

Terms of Service

By using our website or services, you agree to do so only for lawful purposes and not to disrupt the security or operation of our digital platforms. All content on this website, including text, images, and logos, is the property of Surya Plaza Hotel and may not be reproduced without explicit written permission.
