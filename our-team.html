Meet the Heart of Surya Plaza Hotel

Our Team & Our Conduct

It’s About People

At Surya Plaza Hotel, we believe a great stay isn’t just about luxury or location—it’s about people. Every warm greeting, every detail in your room, and every meal served with care is the result of a team that’s passionate about making your time with us unforgettable.

Front Desk & Concierge

Our front desk staff are more than just receptionists—they’re your first point of connection. Whether helping with check-in, arranging transfers, or offering local tips, they’re available 24/7 to assist with a smile.

Housekeeping & Maintenance

Clean rooms and smooth operations don’t happen by accident. Our housekeeping and maintenance teams work quietly behind the scenes to ensure your space is spotless, safe, and serene.

Food & Beverage Team

From chefs to servers, our culinary team is committed to delighting your senses. Everyone plays a role in delivering fresh, flavorful dishes with care, whether you’re starting your day with breakfast or enjoying a late-night meal.

Events & Guest Experience

From romantic getaways to weddings or business events, our guest experience and events team are experts at turning moments into memories. They’ll walk with you through every step, so all you have to do is enjoy the occasion.

Our Culture – Built on Care

We’ve built a hotel culture centered on respect, teamwork, and meaningful connections. These are the values we live by every day.

Kindness

Treating every guest and colleague with warmth and empathy.

Excellence

Always doing our best and going the extra mile in every detail.

Inclusion

Welcoming guests and staff of every background and identity.

Your Role in the Experience

We kindly ask our valued guests to help us maintain a respectful and peaceful environment for everyone by treating staff and fellow guests with consideration and following hotel policies.

Passionate about delivering great hotel experiences? Looking to build a career in a supportive, values-driven environment? We are always looking for talented individuals to join our story.
