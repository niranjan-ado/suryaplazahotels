Pearl Banquet

The Perfect Venue for Every Occasion

A Premier Venue in Sahibganj

Pearl Banquet, located on the fifth floor of Surya Plaza Hotel, is a premier event space designed to host grand celebrations, corporate events, and special gatherings with elegance and perfection. This versatile, 1500 sqft venue features a stylish setting and modern amenities, accommodating 30 to 150 guests for any elegant event.

Whether you are planning a wedding, reception, anniversary, or a business conference, our dedicated team ensures flawless execution and impeccable service, which is why so many of our valued guests return for their most important occasions.

Why Choose Pearl Banquet?

  • Versatile Space: 1500 sqft area adaptable for conferences, receptions, and parties.
  • Flexible Capacity: Comfortably hosts intimate gatherings of 30 or larger events up to 150 guests.
  • Modern Amenities: Equipped with everything you need for a successful event.
  • Impeccable Service: A professional team dedicated to making your event unforgettable.
