Pearl Banquet

The Perfect Venue for Every Occasion

A Premier Venue in Sahibganj

Located on the fifth floor, Pearl Banquet is a premier 1500 sqft event space designed to host grand celebrations, corporate events, and special gatherings with elegance and perfection. This versatile venue features a stylish setting and modern amenities, accommodating 30 to 150 guests.

Whether planning a wedding, reception, or a business conference, our dedicated team ensures flawless execution and impeccable service, making every occasion a memorable one.

Why Choose Pearl Banquet?

  • Versatile Space: 1500 sqft area adaptable for conferences, receptions, and parties.
  • Flexible Capacity: Comfortably hosts intimate gatherings of 30 or larger events up to 150 guests.
  • Modern Amenities: Equipped with audio-visual support for a successful event.
  • Impeccable Service: A professional team dedicated to making your event unforgettable.
