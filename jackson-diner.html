Jackson Diner by Moti Mahal

A Contemporary Fusion of Flavors

A Modern Culinary Experience

Jackson Diner offers a unique blend of classic Indian recipes presented with a contemporary touch. Building on the renowned Moti Mahal legacy, this restaurant provides a vibrant ambiance and a diverse menu that caters to all palates, perfect for a casual yet memorable meal.

Authentic Heritage

Honoring the Moti Mahal legacy with authentic tandoori specialties and signature curries.

Contemporary Flavors

A perfect fusion of classic Indian recipes presented with a modern, approachable touch.

Vibrant Ambiance

A lively and inviting atmosphere that reflects the warm, welcoming essence of Indian hospitality.

Accessible Dining

Offering a balance of high-quality food and service at reasonable prices for an enjoyable experience.

Where Good Food Meets Good Times

From the Moti Mahal Legacy

Jackson Diner proudly serves a variety of iconic North Indian dishes, a testament to its rich culinary heritage.

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

The globally renowned signature dish featuring tender tandoori chicken simmered in a creamy, tomato-based gravy.

Tandoori Chicken

A classic preparation where chicken is marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a tandoor for a smoky, succulent flavor.

Dal Makhani

A hearty lentil dish made from black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked with butter and cream to achieve a rich, velvety texture.

The Original Butter Paneer

The iconic recipe featuring cottage cheese cooked in a rich tomato gravy, finished with cream and butter.
