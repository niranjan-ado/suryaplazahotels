Jackson Diner by Moti Mahal

A Fusion of Traditional & Modern Flavors

A Contemporary Culinary Experience

Jackson Diner by Moti Mahal of India offers a unique blend of classic Indian recipes with a contemporary touch. As part of the renowned Moti Mahal legacy, it stands out with a vibrant ambience and a diverse menu that caters to all palates.

Authentic North Indian Cuisine

Honoring the Moti Mahal legacy with authentic butter chicken and tandoori specialties.

Fusion of Flavors

A perfect blend of classic Indian recipes presented with a contemporary, modern touch.

Vibrant Ambience

A lively, inviting atmosphere that reflects the warm essence of Indian hospitality.

Affordable Fine Dining

Offering a balance of high-quality dining at reasonable prices for a memorable experience.

Signature Dishes from the Moti Mahal Legacy

Jackson Diner proudly serves a variety of iconic North Indian dishes, a testament to its rich culinary heritage.

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

This signature dish features tender pieces of tandoori chicken simmered in a creamy, tomato-based gravy.

Tandoori Chicken

A classic preparation where chicken is marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a tandoor for a smoky flavor.

Dal Makhani

A hearty lentil dish made from black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked with butter and cream to achieve a rich, velvety texture.

The Original Butter Paneer

The iconic recipe featuring cottage cheese cooked in a tomato gravy and finished with cream and butter.
