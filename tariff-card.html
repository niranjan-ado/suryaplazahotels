Surya Plaza Hotel

Located in the central business district of Sahibganj, Surya Plaza Hotel provides well-appointed accommodations for business and leisure travellers. The hotel offers convenient access to the city's commercial hubs, cultural landmarks, and key transportation links.

Booking Policies

  • Check-in / Check-out: The hotel operates on a 24-hour check-in and check-out policy.
  • Additional Occupants: Extra Adult: ₹ 700 + Taxes. Extra Child: ₹ 500 + Taxes.
  • Child Policy: One child up to 12 years of age stays free of charge when sharing existing bedding.
  • Cancellation Policy: Free cancellation is permitted up to 24 hours prior to the scheduled check-in time.
  • Payment: All major credit and debit cards are accepted. Payment is processed via Razorpay.
  • Taxes: All rates are subject to 12% GST. Tariffs are subject to change without prior notice.
