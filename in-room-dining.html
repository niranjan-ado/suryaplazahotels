In-Room Dining

Curated Flavors, Delivered to Your Door

The Best of Our Kitchens, 24/7

For your convenience and comfort, our In-Room Dining service offers a curated selection of signature dishes from our renowned restaurants. Enjoy delectable choices in the privacy of your room, whenever you wish.

How to Order

Please Dial "9" from Your Room's Telephone

Our team is available 24 hours a day to take your order and assist with any dietary requirements.

Chef's Recommendations

A selection of our most popular dishes available for in-room delivery.

From Moti Mahal

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

The signature dish of tender tandoori chicken simmered in a creamy, tomato-based gravy.

Dal Makhani

A hearty lentil dish, slow-cooked with butter and cream to achieve a rich, velvety texture.

The Original Butter Paneer

The iconic recipe featuring cottage cheese cooked in a rich tomato gravy, finished with cream and butter.

From Jackson Diner

Crunchy Chicken Burger

A crispy fried chicken patty with caramelized onion, plum tomatoes, lettuce, and cheese, served with fries.

Pasta Alfredo (Veg)

Pasta with exotic vegetables tossed in a creamy Alfredo sauce, served with garlic bread.

King Club Sandwich (Non-Veg)

A three-layer sandwich with grilled chicken, chicken ham, a fried egg, and cheese, served with fries.
