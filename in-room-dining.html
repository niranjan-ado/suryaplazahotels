In-Room Dining

Curated Flavors, Delivered to Your Door

The Best of Our Kitchens

For your convenience and comfort, our In-Room Dining service offers a curated selection of signature dishes from our renowned restaurants. Enjoy delectable choices from the comfort of your room, whenever the fancy strikes.

Chef's Recommendations

A selection of our most popular dishes available for in-room delivery.

From Moti Mahal

Vegetarian

The Original Butter Paneer

The iconic recipe featuring cottage cheese cooked in a tomato gravy and finished with cream and butter.

Dal Makhani

A hearty lentil dish, slow-cooked with butter and cream to achieve a rich, velvety texture.

Kadhai Paneer

Cottage cheese cooked with onion, bell peppers, and tomato in a traditional Indian wok.

Pindi Chana Masala

White chickpeas cooked in a carom-flavored onion and tomato gravy.

Non-Vegetarian

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

The signature dish of tender tandoori chicken simmered in a creamy, tomato-based gravy.

Tandoori Chicken

A classic preparation where chicken is marinated and cooked in a tandoor for a smoky flavor.

Mutton Rogan Josh

A flavorful curry with tender mutton pieces cooked in a rich, aromatic gravy.

From Jackson Diner

Vegetarian

Pasta Alfredo

Pasta with exotic vegetables tossed in a creamy Alfredo sauce, served with garlic bread.

Crunchy Paneer Burger

A crispy fried cottage cheese patty with grilled onion, tomatoes, and cheese, served with fries.

Tandoori Paneer Tikka Pizza

A thin-crust pizza topped with shredded paneer tikka and mozzarella cheese.

Queen Club Sandwich

A three-layer sandwich with coleslaw, onion, tomato, cucumber, and cheese.

Non-Vegetarian

Crunchy Chicken Burger

A crispy fried chicken patty with caramelized onion, plum tomatoes, lettuce, and cheese, served with fries.

Chicken Tikka Roll

Tandoor-roasted shredded chicken tikka with onion & tri-peppers, stuffed in a soft roomali roti.

King Club Sandwich

A three-layer sandwich with grilled chicken, chicken ham, fried egg, and cheese.
