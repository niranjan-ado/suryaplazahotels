Cancellation & Modification Policy

Fair and Flexible Guidelines for Your Reservation

Our Commitment

At Surya Plaza Hotel, we understand that travel plans can change unexpectedly. Our cancellation and modification policy is designed to be as flexible and fair as possible while ensuring the operational efficiency of our hotel. Please review the following guidelines for managing your reservation.

1. Standard Reservations

  • Free Cancellation: Standard reservations may be cancelled without penalty if the cancellation is made at least **24 hours prior** to the scheduled check-in time (24 Hrs).
  • Late Cancellations: Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled check-in time will incur a cancellation fee equivalent to the first night's room rate plus applicable taxes.
  • No-Show Policy: If a guest does not arrive on the scheduled check-in date without prior notification, the reservation will be classified as a 'No-Show'. The first night's room rate will be charged, and any remaining nights on the reservation will be cancelled.

2. Non-Refundable Reservations

Certain promotional rates or special packages may be designated as "non-refundable." These reservations are offered at a discounted rate and are not eligible for cancellation, modification, or refund. The full amount of the stay will be charged at the time of booking. Please review the rate details carefully before confirming a non-refundable reservation.

3. Group & Event Bookings

For group reservations (defined as 5 or more rooms) and bookings associated with weddings, conferences, or other events at our Pearl Banquet, a separate cancellation policy will apply as outlined in the group booking contract. Please refer to your agreement or contact our events team for specific details.

4. Reservation Modifications

We are pleased to assist with modifying your reservation dates or room category, subject to availability and any potential rate differences.

  • Modification requests should be made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled check-in time to avoid any fees.
  • Changes are contingent upon hotel availability for the new dates or room type requested.

5. Refund Processing

For any reservations eligible for a refund, the amount will be credited to the original method of payment. Please allow 5-10 business days for the transaction to be processed by your financial institution. Eligibility for refunds on early departures is dependent on the rate plan booked.

6. Exceptional Circumstances

We recognize that unforeseen emergencies can occur. In documented cases of medical emergencies, government-imposed travel restrictions, or natural disasters, we are committed to working with our guests on a compassionate and equitable basis. Depending on the situation, this may include a complimentary date change or a credit for a future stay.

Policy at a Glance

  • Standard: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in.
  • No-Show: First night's rate is charged.
  • Non-Refundable Rates: No cancellations or modifications permitted.
  • Modifications: Welcome, subject to availability and 24-hour notice.
  • Refunds: Processed to original payment method in 5-10 business days.

How to Modify or Cancel

To manage your reservation, please use the appropriate channel:

Direct Bookings:
For reservations made via our website or phone, please contact our reservations desk directly.

Third-Party Bookings:
For reservations made via online travel agencies (e.g., Booking.com, Expedia), please manage your booking through their platform.
