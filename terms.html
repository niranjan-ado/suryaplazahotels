General Terms

Welcome to Surya Plaza Hotel. These Terms and Conditions govern your booking and stay at our hotel. By making a reservation, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined below. These terms are designed to ensure a safe, comfortable, and pleasant experience for all our guests.

1. Reservations & Payment

  • Booking Confirmation: All reservations are subject to availability and are considered confirmed only upon receipt of a valid booking confirmation from the hotel.
  • Tariffs: Room rates are as per the published Tariff Card, exclusive of applicable taxes (currently 12% GST). Tariffs are subject to change without prior notice.
  • Payment: We accept all major credit and debit cards. Online payments are securely processed via Razorpay. Depending on the rate plan, pre-payment may be required at the time of booking.

2. Check-in & Check-out

  • Policy: The hotel operates on a 24-hour check-in and check-out basis. Your check-out time will be 24 hours after your check-in time.
  • Identification: In accordance with government regulations, all guests are required to present a valid, government-issued photo identification upon check-in. Foreign nationals must present a valid passport and visa.

3. Cancellation & No-Show Policy

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to our guests. For complete details, please refer to our official Cancellation Policy page. A summary is provided below:

  • Standard Reservations: Free cancellation is permitted up to 24 hours before the scheduled check-in time.
  • Late Cancellations & No-Shows: A fee equivalent to the first night's room rate will be charged for cancellations made within 24 hours or in the case of a no-show.

4. Hotel Policies & Guest Conduct

  • Occupancy: Charges for additional occupants are applicable (Extra Adult: ₹ 700 + Taxes; Extra Child: ₹ 500 + Taxes). One child under 12 years may stay free of charge when using existing bedding.
  • Guest Conduct: Guests are requested to conduct themselves in a respectable manner and to not cause any disturbance to other guests. The hotel reserves the right to request a guest to vacate their room if their conduct is deemed inappropriate.
  • Damages: Guests will be held responsible for any loss or damage to hotel property caused by themselves, their friends, or any person for whom they are responsible.
  • Smoking: Smoking is strictly prohibited in all guest rooms and indoor public areas. A cleaning fee will be charged to guests who violate this policy.
  • Lost & Found: Items left behind will be held for 30 days. Please refer to our Shipping Policy for details on retrieving lost items.

5. Limitation of Liability

The hotel is not responsible for the loss of or damage to any personal valuables left in guest rooms. Secure in-room safes are provided for your convenience. The hotel's liability for any loss or damage will be limited to the amount paid for the services.

6. Governing Law

These terms shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with these terms shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Sahibganj, Jharkhand.
