General Terms

Welcome to Surya Plaza Hotel. These Terms and Conditions govern all bookings and stays at our hotel. By making a reservation, you, the guest, acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined below. These terms are established to ensure a safe, comfortable, and pleasant experience for all our patrons.

1. Reservations & Payment

  • Booking Confirmation: All reservations are subject to availability and are considered confirmed only upon our issuance of a valid booking confirmation.
  • Tariffs: Room rates are as per the published Tariff Card and are exclusive of applicable taxes (currently 12% GST). Tariffs are subject to change without prior notice.
  • Payment: We accept all major credit and debit cards. Online payments are securely processed via Razorpay. Depending on the selected rate plan, pre-payment may be required at the time of booking to secure your reservation.

2. Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in/Check-out Policy: The hotel operates on a 24-hour check-in and check-out policy. Your check-out time is calculated as 24 hours from your time of check-in.
  • Identification: In accordance with government regulations, all guests are required to present valid, government-issued photo identification upon check-in. Foreign nationals must present a valid passport and visa.

3. Cancellation & No-Show Policy

  • Standard Reservations: Free cancellation is permitted up to 24 hours prior to the scheduled check-in time.
  • Late Cancellations & No-Shows: A retention charge equivalent to the first night's room rate plus applicable taxes will be levied for any cancellations made within 24 hours of arrival or in the event of a no-show.
  • For a comprehensive overview, please refer to our official Cancellation Policy.

4. Guest Conduct & Hotel Policies

  • Additional Occupants: Charges for additional occupants are applicable as per the tariff card (Extra Adult: ₹700 + Taxes; Extra Child: ₹500 + Taxes). One child under 12 years may stay free of charge when using existing bedding.
  • Guest Conduct: Guests are requested to conduct themselves in a manner that is respectful of the peace and comfort of other patrons. The hotel reserves the right to request a guest to vacate their room if their conduct is deemed disruptive or inappropriate.
  • Property Damage: Guests will be held financially responsible for any loss or damage to hotel property caused by themselves, their associates, or any person for whom they are responsible.
  • Smoking: In the interest of health and safety, smoking is strictly prohibited in all guest rooms and indoor public areas. A significant cleaning fee will be assessed for any violation of this policy.

5. Limitation of Liability

While the hotel employs robust security measures, we are not responsible for the loss of or damage to any personal valuables. Secure in-room safes are provided for your convenience. The hotel's total liability for any loss or damage shall be limited to the amount paid for the services rendered.

6. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

These terms shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with these terms shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Sahibganj, Jharkhand.
