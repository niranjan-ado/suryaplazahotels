Our Commitment to Your Convenience

Guest Shipping & Delivery Policy

At Surya Plaza Hotel, we aim to provide a seamless experience for our guests. This policy outlines our procedures for handling guest packages, deliveries, and the return of left-behind items to ensure clarity and convenience.

1. Scope of Services

Our concierge and front desk teams are pleased to assist with the following services through trusted third-party carriers:

  • Lost & Found Returns: Facilitating the shipment of personal belongings left at the hotel.
  • Pre-Arrival Deliveries: Receiving and securely storing packages sent on behalf of registered future guests.
  • Outgoing Shipments: Assisting with the dispatch of documents or packages during your stay.

2. Fees and Processing

All shipping and handling fees are the responsibility of the guest. These costs are determined by package dimensions, weight, destination, and the selected service speed. Our team will provide an estimated cost for your approval before proceeding with any shipment.

3. Lost & Found Services

Should you leave an item behind, please contact us at your earliest convenience. Once we have located and verified the item, we will provide you with a shipping cost estimate for its return. Unclaimed items are held for a period of 30 days before being donated to a local charity.

4. Pre-Arrival Packages

If you wish to send packages to the hotel prior to your arrival, please ensure the shipping label clearly includes the full name on the guest reservation and the check-in date. We kindly request that you notify us in advance so we may anticipate the delivery.

5. Prohibited Items

For safety and legal compliance, we are unable to facilitate the shipment of hazardous materials, perishable goods, or any items restricted by local or international shipping regulations. Please consult with our concierge team if you are uncertain about a specific item.

Policy at a Glance

  • All shipping costs are borne by the guest.
  • We assist with shipments via trusted third-party carriers.
  • Lost items are held for 30 days.
  • Please notify us of any pre-arrival packages.
  • Hazardous and perishable items cannot be shipped.

Contact Our Concierge

For any questions regarding shipping or deliveries, please contact our front desk.
