Concierge & Guest Services

Our Commitment to Your Convenience & Peace of Mind

Our Service Promise

At Surya Plaza Hotel, our hospitality extends beyond your stay. Our front desk and concierge teams are pleased to assist with a range of services to ensure your experience is seamless, from handling personal parcels to reuniting you with left-behind belongings.

1. Lost & Found Items

We understand that items can sometimes be left behind. Should you realize you have misplaced an item, please contact our front desk at your earliest convenience. Our team will do its utmost to locate your belongings.

  • Item Retrieval: Once we locate and verify the item, we will provide you with a shipping cost estimate for its secure return via a trusted courier.
  • Holding Period: Unclaimed items are securely stored for a period of 30 days. After this time, they are donated to a local charity.

2. Guest Parcel Services

Whether you need to receive a package before you arrive or send one during your stay, our team is here to help.

  • Receiving Packages: If you wish to send packages to the hotel prior to your arrival, please ensure the shipping label clearly includes the full name on the guest reservation and the check-in date. We kindly request that you notify us in advance so we may anticipate the delivery.
  • Sending Packages: Our concierge can assist with arranging for the dispatch of your documents or packages through a reliable third-party carrier.

3. Policy Details

  • Fees: All shipping and handling fees are the responsibility of the guest. These costs are determined by package dimensions, weight, destination, and the selected courier service.
  • Prohibited Items: For the safety of our guests and staff, and in compliance with shipping regulations, we are unable to handle or ship hazardous materials, perishable goods, or any other items restricted by law.

Key Services

  • Lost & Found Item Return
  • Pre-Arrival Package Receipt
  • Outgoing Parcel Assistance
  • Secure 30-Day Holding Period
