Moti Mahal Tandoori Trail

A Legacy of Royal Cuisine

A Legendary Culinary Heritage

As part of the iconic brand that introduced Butter Chicken and Dal Makhani to the world, Moti Mahal offers a dining experience founded on a rich legacy and signature flavors. We proudly bring this authentic taste of North Indian, Mughlai, and Tandoori cuisine to Sahibganj.

Authentic Tandoori

Signature dishes cooked to perfection in traditional clay ovens for an unmatched smoky flavor.

Royal Mughlai Cuisine

Indulge in a rich selection of aromatic curries, slow-cooked biryanis, and decadent gravies.

Elegant Ambiance

A sophisticated yet inviting setting, ideal for family dinners, business meetings, and special occasions.

Exceptional Service

Our dedicated team is committed to providing a warm and memorable dining experience for every guest.

An Atmosphere of Distinction

Our Signature Dishes

Experience the iconic flavors that have defined North Indian cuisine for generations.

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

The dish that made Moti Mahal a global icon. Tender tandoori chicken simmered in a silken, creamy tomato gravy.

Tandoori Chicken

A timeless classic. Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then roasted in a traditional tandoor for a smoky, succulent finish.

Dal Makhani

A harmonious blend of black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked with butter and cream to achieve a rich, velvety texture.

Mutton Rogan Josh

Tender mutton pieces cooked in a deeply aromatic gravy, renowned for its rich flavor and best enjoyed with fresh naan bread.
