A Legacy of Hospitality

Discover the story, mission, and values that define the Surya Plaza experience.

From Care to Comfort

Our journey into hospitality is rooted in a legacy of care. Inspired by our experience in the healthcare sector with the esteemed Surya Super Speciality Hospital, we founded Surya Plaza Hotel on a core principle: to provide impeccable service, genuine comfort, and a deep sense of wellbeing to every guest.

This unique philosophy guides everything we do, ensuring that a stay with us is not just a visit, but a truly restorative experience.

Our Commitment to You

Three core pillars define our promise of an exceptional stay.

Exceptional Comfort

From thoughtfully designed rooms with plush bedding to spacious suites and family apartments, every space is crafted to be your personal sanctuary.

Culinary Distinction

Through our partnership with the legendary Moti Mahal brand, we offer an authentic and diverse dining experience, from royal Tandoori trails to casual diner delights.

Memorable Occasions

With our elegant Pearl Banquet hall and dedicated events team, we provide the perfect venue and impeccable service to make your special events truly unforgettable.
