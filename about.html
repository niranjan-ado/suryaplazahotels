A Legacy of Hospitality

Discover the story and values that define the Surya Plaza experience.

From Care to Comfort

Our journey into hospitality is rooted in a legacy of care. Inspired by our experience in the healthcare sector with the esteemed Surya Super Speciality Hospital, we founded Surya Plaza Hotel on a core principle: to provide impeccable service, genuine comfort, and a deep sense of wellbeing to every guest.

This unique philosophy guides everything we do, ensuring that a stay with us is not just a visit, but a truly restorative experience.

Our Commitment to You

Three core pillars define our promise of an exceptional stay.

Exceptional Comfort

From thoughtfully designed rooms to spacious suites and apartments, every space is crafted to be your personal sanctuary.

Culinary Distinction

Through our partnership with the legendary Moti Mahal brand, we offer an authentic and diverse dining experience for every palate.

Memorable Occasions

With our elegant Pearl Banquet and dedicated events team, we provide the perfect venue to make your special events truly unforgettable.

The Heart of Our Hotel

A great stay is not just about luxury or location—it’s about people. Every warm greeting, thoughtful detail, and meal served with care is the result of a team that is passionate about making your time with us exceptional.

Front Desk & Concierge

Available 24/7, our front desk team members are your first point of connection, ready to assist with check-in, local tips, and any request with a smile.

Housekeeping Team

Working diligently behind the scenes, our housekeeping professionals ensure that your personal space is always spotless, comfortable, and serene.

Culinary Team

From our chefs to our servers, the culinary team is committed to delighting your senses with fresh, flavorful dishes, morning, noon, and night.

Experience Our Hospitality

Allow us to make your visit to Sahibganj truly memorable. Explore our accommodations or get in touch with our team to plan your stay.
