Our Commitment to Your Comfort

We are dedicated to providing an exceptional stay, distinguished by the following conveniences.

Spacious Accommodations

Well-appointed rooms, suites, and apartments, many featuring private balconies and river views.

Renowned Dining

Home to the famous Moti Mahal restaurant, serving authentic Indian and Chinese cuisine.

Central Location

Modern Amenities

Enjoy complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, 24/7 room service, and individual climate control in every room.

Our Story

Surya Plaza Hotel was founded with a clear vision: to provide a standard of hospitality in Sahibganj built on genuine warmth and impeccable service. Our commitment is to ensure every guest—whether traveling for business, leisure, or with family—experiences a stay that is both comfortable and memorable.
